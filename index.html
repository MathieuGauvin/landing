ArtConnect

Connecting all art lovers

ArtConnect is a revolutionary art sales startup, offering a global online platform that eliminates geographical and financial barriers for art lovers, artists and collectors. With features such as a virtual art marketplace, participatory funding for emerging artists, online art appreciation courses, and guaranteed authenticity via blockchain, ArtConnect democratizes art by making it accessible to all while creating a community of art lovers. Join us in our mission to revolutionize the art industry and make art accessible to everyone, wherever you are in the world. ArtConnect: where art meets the world..
